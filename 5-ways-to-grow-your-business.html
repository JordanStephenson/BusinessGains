5 Ways to Grow your Fitness Business

Last updated Nov. 1, 2017


Are you struggling to become a successful fitness business entrepreneur looking for a magic wand to turn your small business into a giant enterprise with huge financial turnovers? In today’s digital world, your laptop and smartphone are the magic wands you ought to be looking for. To establish and successfully run a fitness center, planning and designing your marketing strategies in accordance with the current trends and technologies is the first and foremost step. The world is going more digital every day, so you need to be more up to date in developing your marketing strategies to ensure maximum output of your efforts. The business of fitness training is highly competitive in today’s world as more and more fitness trainers are emerging on the scene trying to make a place for themselves. In order to build a successful fitness training franchise, you need to be more innovative and strongly committed to your marketing strategies. This article will introduce you to five of the current trends of marketing that will boost your business. Remember, these tips will give you only a basic guideline to follow, you will have to adjust and plan your business tactics in accordance with your individual requirements.


Digital Marketing Via Social Media


Social media has attained the status of the kingmaker in the modern world and, therefore, it is one of the keys to success for your fitness business. Social media is the most widely used medium of communication and statistics also endorse this fact. Approximately 97% of adult population in most countries is linked with one or more of social networking sites. It has been estimated that nearly 70 percent of the social media users make purchase of brands marketed on social media. Use social media FOR FREE to reach your targeted and potential customers to expand your clientele. Making a choice for an appropriate social media platform to project your fitness training business can, sometimes, be a really confusing task especially if you are beginner in this domain. As a newcomer, however, the most suitable social media platforms to begin your digital marketing with are Facebook, YouTube and Instagram. You may use many social media platforms simultaneously but you need to prioritize only one as the epicenter of your digital marketing efforts. You also need to switch to online scheduling service for appointments from traditional procedure of on call appointments because people these day prefer making effortless one click online appointments over following routine appointment procedures. Using online appointment procedure not only helps you conduct online classes but also gives you immediate estimate of number of registered clients. You can also use social media platforms to get online payments for your services as well. In the view of these facts and figure, it is strongly recommended that you actively use social media platforms to grow your fitness business.


Paid Advertisement


You might have to invest in social media advertisement to establish your brand in the digital world. Effective social media advertising will rapidly boost your fitness training business by increasing your online customers. Are you reluctant to use online advertisement service due to possible financial strain? Well, it does not cost much at all. You can use Facebook and Instagram advertising service by spending just 1$ a day. Investing on Instagram advertisement especially gives you a quick and good return of your money. While using social media advertisement, try to focus areas and people having a particular interest in your product such as localities having large proportion of old age people. You also need to make your ads attractive and informative by using eye-catching high quality photos and highlighting new techniques of fitness training. You can also use your personal website to redirect people who visit your website towards your Facebook and Instagram ads. So get ready and get down to do some serious digital fitness marketing.


Create a Website


You can create your own website or if you already have one, keep it updated and use SEO techniques to make it more reachable for people around you. A website with effective SEO is read by more people and attracts and creates more customers. The problem with SEO is that it is not in much trend as compared to social media ads and most people who are related to fitness training business are not much informed about its effectiveness in marketing a fitness training business; however, with SEO (search engine optimization), you can increase the visibility of your website in all the major search engines and, thus, can increase online traffic of your target customers on your website. Higher online traffic on your website means more chances of getting online customers. SEO makes your website appear on the top of the search list for the keyword: fitness training. If you are not much skilled in the science of SEO, hire a professional to help you out. SEO is a very potent but highly overlooked technique in the online marketing of fitness training business and you can, if not others, utilize its full potential to maximize your online clientele.

Increase readership


You may also increase your website readership by adding some FREE educational content or an interesting blog on a related topic. You can upload blogs on recent innovations, researches and techniques in the field of fitness training also you can add materials related to healthy diets, food supplements, etc. to make your website more attractive and informative. Your testimonials are your biggest assets as they reflect on the achievements and success you have made in your overall career and present you as a reliable fitness trainer. Testimonials increase your credibility in the eyes of your current and potential customers since people give weightage to other people’s opinions about a business than your own claims. Try to get testimonials from your current as well as previous clients and keep uploading them on your website to make your potential clients convinced about your professionalism and expertise. More importantly, you have to use updated and innovative personal training services to draw in more customers.

Quality service


Ultimately, your marketing strategies will prove fruitful only if you always provide your customers a quality service and best customer experience. Keep adding new training methods and equipment to retain your cliental which is often more challenging than creating one. Always keep in touch with your clients and pay heed to their problems and make every effort to resolve them. You should always remember that in order to be a successful fitness trainer, you need to be result oriented while dealing with your clients because your results and proofs of successful transformations of your clients are the keys to unlock a successful career as a fitness trainer. Most importantly, while running a fitness training business, try to build relationships with your clients rather than just marketing your brand on various platforms and channels. Use social media effectively to build relationships with your clients and other professional trainers to enhance your social sphere and thereby your business. Having understood all the ways of boosting your fitness business, you should always keep in mind the fact that dedication and timely action are the foundations behind the success of any of these strategies. So always stay focused and committed to your work..
